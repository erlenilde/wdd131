About Me

My name is Erlenilde Santos and people call me Santos. I was born in Brazil, Minas Gerais estate. I'm married to the love of my life, Stéphane Santos, and I have 3 children, Daniel, Pedro Henrique and Joao Victor. I run my own business in solar panel sales country-wide. I'm 44 and very excited for being studying in a college again. I hope I can handle my schedule well, to keep all the responsabilities working.

Brazil is the 5th largest country by land area and the 10th largest economy in the world. The country is rich in sunlight, water, agriculture, and preserved forests. It has a vibrant, joyful, and hardworking people. In some areas, it suffers greatly from violence due to drug trafficking. We have many holidays here, such as Carnival, which can last up to a week! In the middle of the year, the Northeast of the country celebrates the so-called 'June Celebrations,' which can last up to 30 days!
